# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 13, 2026**

Rustymail is a desktop email client that runs locally on your device. Your data never leaves your computer.

## Data We Access

When you connect an email account, Rustymail accesses:

- **Email messages** — To display your inbox, sent mail, drafts, and other folders
- **Calendar events** — If you enable calendar integration
- **Contacts** — If you enable contact discovery

## How Data Is Used

All data is used exclusively for the purposes of the email client:

- Displaying and managing your email
- Providing search across your messages
- Detecting subscription/newsletter senders
- AI features (optional, runs entirely on your device)

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device** in a SQLite database. No data is sent to Rustymail servers. We do not operate backend servers that process or store your email.

## Data Retention and Deletion

You control your data at all times:

- Delete your account from Rustymail at any time from Settings
- Uninstalling the application removes all locally stored data
- Your email remains on your provider's servers (Gmail, Outlook, etc.) per their retention policies

## No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer your data to any third party.

## No Advertising

We do not scan your email content for advertising purposes. Rustymail does not display advertisements.

## No Human Access

We have no ability to access your email data. Rustymail is a local application — there is no server-side component that could allow human review of your data.

## No Telemetry on Email Content

Rustymail does not collect telemetry on email content, sender/recipient information, or message metadata.

## AI Features

AI features (summarization, compose assistance, action item extraction) run entirely on your device using a local language model. No email content is sent to external AI services.

## Google Limited Use Compliance

Rustymail's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the Limited Use requirements.

## Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy as the application evolves. Changes will be reflected in the application and in the public repository.
